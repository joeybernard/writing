Mapping Your GIS Data

We've already looked at other GIS applications available on Linux. Programs like GRASS and qgis provide a full set of tools to do GIS. Sometimes, this is really overkill. You may just want to display the data geographically and create a map. In these cases, there is thuban. 

If you don't already have data of your own, there are sources of public domain GIS data available online. In the links below, you will find a few sites to get you started.
